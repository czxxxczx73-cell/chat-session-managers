# Code of Conduct

Chat Session Managers welcomes people who want to improve local-first tools for Codex, Claude Code, and Grok users.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Assume good intent while discussing ideas, bugs, and tradeoffs.
- Keep feedback focused on the work rather than the person.
- Protect privacy: never post real conversation data, credentials, usernames, or personal paths.
- Respect maintainers' and contributors' time, decisions, and boundaries.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, personal attacks, deliberate disclosure of private information, spam, and repeated disruptive behavior are not acceptable in project spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove content and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior conflicts with this policy. Decisions should be proportionate, documented where appropriate, and focused on keeping the project safe and productive.

For a private project-specific report, use the repository's [private security advisory form](https://github.com/czxxxczx73-cell/chat-session-managers/security/advisories/new) and begin the title with `Conduct report`. For abuse that violates GitHub-wide policies, use GitHub's report-abuse tools.

This policy applies to repository issues, pull requests, discussions, review comments, and other project-managed spaces.
